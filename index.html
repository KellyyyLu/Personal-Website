About

I was born in Chiayi, Taiwan but grew up in Guangzhou, China. Not growing up in my hometown had a major influence on me. I traveled a lot between the two places that I could call "home". Traveling became a part of me. The rich culture and different styles of building around the world calls me to discover them. I am an explorer in culture and architecture.
